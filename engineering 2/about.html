About Us

Our Story

Mango Healthcare Center was opened in 1962 by the Sisters of Mercy, a Catholic Order of Nuns from Ireland. The hospital has grown significantly since then, expanding its services and facilities to meet the needs of the community.

Between 1972 and 1986, the hospital achieved several milestones, including:

  • Chartering as a School of Midwifery.
  • Opening a consultant's block for specialized consultations.
  • Establishing pharmacy, physiotherapy, and laboratory services.
  • Launching a counselling center for patients and staff.
  • Expanding the Casualty, Accident and Emergency Department.

Healthcare Philosophy

In accordance with the philosophy of the Sisters of Mercy, we aim to reach out to all community members irrespective of race, color, or creed. As a non-profit institution, we strive to provide modern healthcare facilities while reinvesting any surplus into our mission.

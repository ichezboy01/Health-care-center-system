Research at Mango Healthcare Center

Workshop Overview

The MHC Introduction to Clinical Research workshop was successfully held from March 27 to March 30, 2023, at Melili Hotel in South B, Nairobi. A total of 53 staff were trained through highly engaging and interactive sessions, attaining Good Clinical Practice (GCP) certification.

The training facilitators included:

  • Dr. Kevin Rombosia (DMS, MHC)
  • Dr. Olive Akunga (Physician & Clinical Epidemiologist, MHC)
  • James Kahiga (Nursing Tutor & Medical Researcher, MHC)
  • Alex Mutuku (Biostatistician)

Through the MHC Research Committee, we aim to schedule similar workshops annually to empower our research champions with relevant knowledge and skills.

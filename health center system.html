About Us

Mango Health Center is a leading medical service provider in Kenya, with a vast network of Outpatient Centres across the country and growing.

We take pride in our experienced medical and management teams, backed by internationally approved disease management protocols and cutting-edge information technology systems. This ensures consistent treatment outcomes for our clients, setting us apart from other providers in the region.

Our comprehensive value chain, strong focus on clinical risk management, and emphasis on preventive care make us stand out. We are dedicated to delivering quality, affordable care sustainably to all our valued clients.

Trust in Mango Health for compassionate and exceptional outpatient healthcare services. Your well-being is our priority, and we are committed to providing you with the best possible care. Welcome to a healthier tomorrow with us.

Excellence in Patient Care

Our History, Mission, & Vision

Established in 1984, Mango Health has a long, robust history. Originally focused on accident and medical evacuation by road and air, we have since expanded to offer a diverse portfolio of services. Throughout our evolution, we have always retained our values of quality, innovation, and excellence.

Our Mission

We provide healthcare solutions that make a difference to life.

Our Vision

To be the provider of choice for innovative and quality healthcare solutions in Kenya.
